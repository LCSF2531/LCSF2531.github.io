About DeepFlow Enterprises Inc.

DeepFlow Enterprises is a proudly Canadian clean-tech company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario. Founded in 2020 with a clear mission: to make everyday home life more sustainable without compromising performance or style.

We design and manufacture premium eco-friendly home appliances, led by our flagship Jovexa EcoMist™ series of ultra-efficient, whisper-quiet dishwashers. By combining cutting-edge water-recycling technology, AI-driven wash cycles, and 100% recyclable materials, DeepFlow helps Canadian families reduce water usage by up to 60% and energy consumption by up to 45% compared to conventional models — all while delivering sparkling results every time.
